Privacy Policy for Loop Speed Screenshot for YouTube

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

1. DATA COLLECTION

This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any user data. We do not track your browsing history, video watching habits, or any personal information.

2. PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED

  • activeTab: Required to inject controls into YouTube video pages when you're actively viewing them
  • scripting: Required to add control buttons to YouTube video player interface
  • storage: Used ONLY to save your feature preferences (loop on/off, screenshot on/off, speed control on/off) locally on your device
  • Host Permission (*.youtube.com/*): Required to operate exclusively on YouTube pages

3. LOCAL STORAGE

The extension stores only your feature preferences using Chrome's sync storage. This data:

  • Stays on your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Can be cleared by removing the extension
  • Contains only three boolean values (on/off for each feature)

4. SCREENSHOTS

Screenshots you capture are:

  • Saved directly to your Downloads folder
  • Copied to your clipboard (if you grant permission)
  • Never uploaded or transmitted anywhere
  • Completely under your control

5. NO THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

This extension does not:

  • Use analytics or tracking
  • Connect to external servers
  • Share data with third parties
  • Display advertisements
  • Monitor your activity

6. NO DATA TRANSMISSION

All functionality operates entirely within your browser. No data leaves your computer. The extension has no backend servers and makes no external network requests.

7. CONTACT

For questions or concerns, contact: dev.duo777@gmail.com

8. CHANGES TO PRIVACY POLICY

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in extension updates with clear notification to users.
